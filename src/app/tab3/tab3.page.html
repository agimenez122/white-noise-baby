A baby white noise app is a digital tool that plays soothing white noise sounds to help parents and caregivers calm and relax babies. Baby white noise refers to sounds similar to what a newborn hears in the mother's womb, such as the sound of a heartbeat, blood flow, and other gentle and continuous sounds.

This app offers a variety of sound options for parents to choose from, such as the soft hum of a machine, the sound of rain, a fan's noise, or the gentle waves of the ocean. These sounds help create a calm and familiar environment for the baby, as they mimic the sounds they heard while in the womb.

Baby white noise is especially useful in helping newborns fall asleep and maintain a deeper and more prolonged sleep. The constant and soothing sound can help block out other external noises that may disturb the baby and create a more serene sleep environment.
